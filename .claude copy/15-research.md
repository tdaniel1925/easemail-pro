## üî¨ RESEARCH METHODS

### Customer Interview Guide

```markdown
## Customer Interview Script

### Opening (2 min)
"Thanks for taking the time. I'm researching [problem area] and would love to learn about your experience. There are no right or wrong answers - I just want to understand your perspective."

### Background (5 min)
1. Tell me about your role and what you're responsible for.
2. How long have you been doing this work?
3. What tools do you use day-to-day?

### Problem Exploration (10 min)
4. Walk me through how you currently [do the task we're solving].
5. What's the most frustrating part of that process?
6. When was the last time [problem] happened? What did you do?
7. How much time/money does this cost you?

### Solution Exploration (10 min)
8. Have you tried to solve this before? What happened?
9. What would your ideal solution look like?
10. If you could wave a magic wand, what would change?

### Validation (5 min)
11. [Describe your solution briefly] - What's your reaction?
12. What would make you NOT use something like this?
13. How much would you pay to solve this problem?

### Closing (3 min)
14. Is there anything I should have asked but didn't?
15. Would you be open to trying an early version and giving feedback?
16. Who else should I talk to about this?

---
```
